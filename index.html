"Species Biodiversity Utilizing eDNA Analysis in Mill Branch Creek"
Neizer, Courtney-Grace; Dunoyer, Luc A.; Gibbs, Melinda

▼ Abstract (click to expand)

This research paper highlights the inspiring journey of Dr. Hayat Sindi as an example of how great an impact a role model can have in the scope of education and personal growth. This research examines the structural components of Dr. Hayat Sindi’s life, through a narrative analysis approach. Different online sources, including websites, interviews, databases, and talks are studied to emphasize the legacy of Dr. Hayat Sindi’s journey in shaping educational narratives and aspirations for the advancement of society, showcasing the broader importance of such role models in inspiring future generations.
